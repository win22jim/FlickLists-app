# FlickLists

**Movie & TV Watchlist Tracker for iOS**

FlickLists is the free way to track your own movie and TV watchlist and figure out what to watch with the people you watch with — your partner, your roommates, the whole group chat. Track shows episode by episode, remember who watched what, and never argue about what to put on again.
